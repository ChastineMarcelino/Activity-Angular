About Us



BigBrew Milktea is a fast-growing beverage brand in the Philippines, known for its delicious yet affordable milk teas, fruit teas, iced coffees, and premium drinks. With a mission to provide "big taste at a bit price, " BigBrew ensures that customers enjoy high-quality beverages without spending too much. Whether you’re craving a classic milk tea, a refreshing fruit tea, or a rich coffee blend, BigBrew has a wide variety of flavors to satisfy every taste bud.


One of the key reasons for BigBrew’s popularity is its commitment to quality ingredients. Each cup is made with carefully selected tea leaves, real fruit extracts, and premium coffee beans, ensuring a flavorful and refreshing experience. Their signature milk teas come with chewy tapioca pearls, while their fruit teas are infused with crystal sinkers for an added burst of texture. Every drink is crafted with precision to maintain consistency in taste and quality.


BigBrew stands out not only because of its affordability but also because of its creative and diverse menu. From best-selling flavors like Wintermelon, Dark Choco, and Matcha Milk Tea to unique offerings like BLACKPINK (dark chocolate and red velvet) and Kape KMJS (caramel macchiato with espresso shot), the brand continuously innovates to keep up with the latest trends in the beverage industry. Customers can also customize their drinks by adjusting the sugar level and adding extra toppings.


Beyond great drinks, BigBrew takes pride in its customer-focused approach. With numerous branches nationwide, the brand ensures accessibility and convenience for its growing fan base. Their friendly staff and quick service make every visit enjoyable, whether you're ordering for takeout, delivery, or dining in. BigBrew also engages with its customers through social media, offering exciting promos, discounts, and giveaways.


As BigBrew continues to expand, it remains dedicated to bringing happiness in every cup. The brand’s combination of affordability, quality, and variety makes it a top choice for milk tea lovers across the country. Whether you’re a longtime fan or a first-time customer, BigBrew promises a satisfying experience with every sip. So, grab a cup today and discover why BigBrew is loved by so many!
